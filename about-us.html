About Purana Sathi

Connecting old friends, building new memories.

Who We Are

Purana Sathi is more than just a platform; it's a bridge to your past and a gateway to your future network. We are a student-centric community dedicated to helping you rediscover lost connections with schoolmates and manage your academic network with ease. Whether you're looking for an old friend or organizing student records, we provide the tools to make it happen seamlessley.

Our Vision

We envision a world where no friendship is lost to time. By leveraging modern technology, we aim to create a secure and vibrant ecosystem where every student can stay connected. Our goal is to empower students with a reliable platform for record management and social discovery, fostering a lifelong community of learners and friends.

Meet the Founder

The visionary behind Purana Sathi, dedicated to bringing students together and simplifying academic networking. With a passion for technology and community building, they created this platform to ensure no connection is left behind.
